Ambitions resolve techniques

Description:

  1. Think about what kind of global goal do you want to reach with this action? Like, "I want to be a high payed programmer, thats why i need to do this html lesson".
  2. Try to understand that this particular action is not leading to your global goal. It is just a small step and there is no guaranties that you reach your goal.
  3. Concentrate on doing action itself. The goal is to do the task, thats all. Your ambition is irrelevant, you just need to complete the task and feel good about yourself.
  4. If you feel less resistance, do the task. If not, try next technique.
