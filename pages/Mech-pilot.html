Mech pilot technique if nothing helps, but it must be done

Description:

  1. If nothing works, go and try helicopter view. Imagine that you are a meha pilot and your body is your meha.
  2. Imagine your body and your mind is just a tools or weapons of your meha. You can take to account, what they "saying" to you, but you also can ignore it.
  3. Tell to yourself that you can do whatever you want in this state for 10 minutes without any damage to your meha. This is safe no matter condition of the meha.
  4. Start to do your task for 10 minutes. If after 10 minutes you still feel great resistance, you need rest. Stop doing task and take a long break. Like day or two.
